# 🚀 WaitLess – Smart Hospital Queue System

> **“No more chaos. No more waiting blindly.”**

---

## 🧠 Problem

Hospital waiting rooms are still stuck in the past:

* ❌ No visibility of queue
* ❌ Long unpredictable wait times
* ❌ No system transparency
* ❌ Frustration for patients

---

## 💡 Solution

**WaitLess** transforms hospital queues into a **real-time digital experience**.

With a simple scan or login, patients can:

* 📱 View their **live queue position**
* ⏱️ Get **estimated waiting time**
* 🏥 Track **doctor availability**
* 🔔 Stay updated without physically waiting

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 Patient Side

* 🔐 Login / Register
* 🏥 Select hospital
* 📅 Book appointments
* 🎫 Get digital token
* 📊 Live queue tracking

---
